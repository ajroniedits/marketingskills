## Where to Stay in Saint-Martin

Saint-Martin and Sint Maarten offer several distinct villa areas, each with a different rhythm. CA Real Estate Agency can help you choose the location that best fits your group, your plans, and the type of island experience you want.

### Les Terres Basses

Les Terres Basses is one of the island's most sought-after villa areas for privacy, space, and refined residential settings. It is often a strong fit for guests who want a peaceful luxury base, larger villas, beautiful outdoor areas, and easy access to some of the island's most memorable beaches.

If your priority is a private villa experience with room for family or friends, Les Terres Basses is often one of the first areas to consider.

### Lowlands

Lowlands offers a calm, upscale setting near the French and Dutch border. Guests often choose this area for its villa atmosphere, convenient positioning, and access to beaches, dining, and island services without feeling too removed.

It can be a good fit for travelers who want a balance of privacy, comfort, and practical access during their stay.

### Cupecoy

Cupecoy is well suited to guests who want a more connected stay on the Dutch side of the island. The area offers access to restaurants, nightlife, shops, and beaches, making it appealing for travelers who want convenience alongside a luxury villa base.

For groups that plan to move around the island often, Cupecoy can be a practical and lively choice.

### Marigot

Marigot brings guests closer to the French side's dining, markets, marina access, and local character. It can suit travelers who want a stay with a stronger sense of place and easy access to French-side experiences.

For guests who enjoy restaurants, local shops, and a more town-connected rhythm, Marigot may be worth exploring.

### Simpson Bay

Simpson Bay is a strong option for travelers who want energy, convenience, and access to restaurants, boating, beaches, and nightlife. It is especially practical for groups who want to enjoy a full island itinerary with easy access to activities.

If your Saint-Martin stay includes boat days, dining out, and a more active schedule, Simpson Bay may be a natural fit.

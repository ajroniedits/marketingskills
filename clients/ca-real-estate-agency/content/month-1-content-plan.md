# Month 1 SEO Content Plan: CA Real Estate Agency

**Client:** CA Real Estate Agency  
**Site:** https://carealestateagency.com/  
**Plan date:** 2026-05-22  
**Sources:** `../month-1-action-plan.md`, `../audits/initial-seo-audit.md`, `../product-marketing.md`  
**Language:** English only  
**Package scope:** 1 blog article and 2 location or service pages

## Content Objective

Month 1 content should turn the audit's highest-potential content gaps into search-ready assets that support qualified villa rental inquiries and property sales conversations.

The audit found that strategic location pages have SEO potential but need unique content, while core commercial/service pages need clearer search intent, metadata, H1s, trust signals, and internal links. This content plan focuses on three pages that can strengthen the site's commercial SEO foundation without creating unnecessary index bloat.

The final Month 1 content scope is exactly three assets: **1 blog article** and **2 location or service pages**.

## Priority Themes

- **Luxury villa rentals:** Capture high-intent searches from travelers comparing villa options, island fit, and location quality.
- **Property sales:** Support buyers, sellers, and investors looking for trusted local real estate guidance.
- **Geographic focus:** Saint-Martin, Saint-Barthelemy, and Anguilla.
- **Trust and local expertise:** Emphasize boutique service, founder-led credibility, curated listings, and local island guidance.
- **English-only SEO:** Use one canonical English version of each page. Avoid mixed-language page bodies unless a separate multilingual strategy is approved later.

## Deliverable 1: Location/Service Page

### Luxury Villa Rentals in Saint-Martin

**Recommended URL:** `/luxury-villa-rentals-saint-martin/`  
**Primary keyword:** luxury villa rentals Saint-Martin  
**Secondary keywords:** Saint-Martin villa rentals, luxury villas in Saint-Martin, Sint Maarten villa rentals, Caribbean luxury villa rentals  
**Search intent:** Visitors want a curated way to find a reliable luxury villa, understand where to stay, and contact a local expert for availability or recommendations.  
**Priority:** Highest, because villa rentals are a confirmed business focus and Saint-Martin is the agency's strongest local credibility signal.  
**Recommended length:** 900-1,200 words plus featured listings and FAQs.

### Recommended Metadata

**Title tag:** Luxury Villa Rentals in Saint-Martin | CA Real Estate Agency

**Meta description:** Explore curated luxury villa rentals in Saint-Martin with CA Real Estate Agency. Get local guidance, refined service, and support from search to stay.

**H1:** Luxury Villa Rentals in Saint-Martin

### Page Angle

Position CA Real Estate Agency as a boutique local partner for travelers who want a villa that fits their group, location preferences, and desired island experience. The page should reduce uncertainty by explaining how the agency curates villas, guides guests by neighborhood, and supports the stay beyond the booking request.

### Recommended Page Structure

1. **Hero section**
   - Lead with curated luxury villa rentals in Saint-Martin.
   - Include a direct inquiry CTA such as "Request Villa Availability" or "Find Your Saint-Martin Villa."
   - Mention refined service, local guidance, and curated inventory.

2. **Why rent a villa in Saint-Martin**
   - Explain the appeal of privacy, space, views, beaches, and access to both French and Dutch island experiences.
   - Keep the language polished and practical, not generic travel copy.

3. **Where to stay in Saint-Martin**
   - Add short sections for priority areas where inventory supports it:
     - Les Terres Basses
     - Lowlands
     - Cupecoy
     - Marigot
     - Simpson Bay
   - Link to existing or future location pages when available.

4. **Featured luxury villas**
   - Include selected rental listings with property name, bedroom count, location, distinguishing feature, and CTA.
   - Prioritize listings with strong descriptions, images, and availability paths.

5. **Concierge support for your stay**
   - Briefly introduce private chef, boat rental, car rental, restaurants, and beach clubs.
   - Link to the relevant service pages where they are indexable and improved.

6. **Why book with CA Real Estate Agency**
   - Use founder-led and boutique positioning.
   - Mention founded in 2019 if appropriate.
   - Use verified testimonial language such as "in good hands," "easy and stress free," and "above and beyond."
   - Do not invent metrics or use unverified proof numbers.

7. **FAQs**
   - What areas of Saint-Martin are best for luxury villa rentals?
   - How far in advance should I book a Saint-Martin villa?
   - Can CA Real Estate Agency help with services during the stay?
   - Are villas available for families, groups, and special occasions?
   - How do I request availability or a recommendation?

### Internal Links

- Link from homepage hero or rental section to this page.
- Link from this page to featured villa listings.
- Link to priority location pages such as Les Terres Basses when improved.
- Link to concierge pages that support the rental experience.
- Link to contact page and inquiry/booking actions.
- Link from the comparison blog article back to this page.

### Content Notes

- Use English consistently, including listing excerpts shown on this page.
- Add descriptive image alt text such as `Luxury villa terrace in Saint-Martin with ocean view`.
- If this page replaces or upgrades an existing rental hub, preserve the stronger URL if it already has links or rankings.

## Deliverable 3: Blog Article

### Saint-Martin vs Saint-Barthelemy vs Anguilla: Which Island Is Right for Your Villa Stay or Property Search?

**Recommended URL:** `/blog/saint-martin-vs-saint-barthelemy-vs-anguilla/`  
**Primary keyword:** Saint-Martin vs Saint-Barthelemy vs Anguilla  
**Secondary keywords:** Saint-Martin or Saint-Barts, Saint-Martin or Anguilla, best Caribbean island for villa rental, Caribbean villa rental destinations, where to buy Caribbean property  
**Search intent:** Visitors are comparing islands before choosing where to rent, buy, or inquire.  
**Priority:** Medium, because it supports both core commercial pages and helps CA Real Estate Agency own comparison-stage questions.  
**Recommended length:** 1,200-1,600 words.

### Recommended Metadata

**Title tag:** Saint-Martin vs Saint-Barthelemy vs Anguilla: Which Island Is Right for You?

**Meta description:** Compare Saint-Martin, Saint-Barthelemy, and Anguilla for luxury villa stays and Caribbean property searches, with local guidance from CA Real Estate Agency.

**H1:** Saint-Martin vs Saint-Barthelemy vs Anguilla: Which Island Is Right for You?

### Article Angle

Use this article as a practical comparison guide, not a generic destination roundup. The reader should leave with a clearer sense of which island fits their travel style, property goals, group needs, and desire for local support.

### Recommended Outline

1. **Introduction**
   - Acknowledge that all three islands can suit luxury travelers and property seekers, but each offers a different experience.
   - Introduce CA Real Estate Agency's role as a local guide for villas and real estate.

2. **Quick comparison**
   - Saint-Martin: versatile, locally rooted, good for travelers who want variety, dining, beaches, and villa choice.
   - Saint-Barthelemy: ultra-refined, polished, privacy and prestige-driven.
   - Anguilla: quiet, beach-led, calm, and suited to privacy-focused stays.

3. **Best for villa rentals**
   - Compare group trips, family stays, special occasions, repeat visitors, and concierge needs.
   - Link to the Saint-Martin villa rental page.

4. **Best for property buyers**
   - Compare lifestyle fit, market familiarity, access, privacy, and long-term use.
   - Link to the luxury real estate page.

5. **Best for sellers and villa owners**
   - Briefly explain representation, property presentation, and attracting qualified guests or buyers.
   - Link to `/sell-with-us/`.

6. **How to choose the right island**
   - Ask questions around group size, preferred pace, restaurant/beach access, privacy, budget range, and ownership goals.

7. **Work with a local boutique agency**
   - Close with trust, curation, and "in good hands" positioning.
   - CTA to contact the agency.

### Internal Links

- Link to `Luxury Villa Rentals in Saint-Martin`.
- Link to `Luxury Real Estate in Saint-Martin, Saint-Barthelemy & Anguilla`.
- Link to `/sell-with-us/`.
- Link to `/contact-us/`.
- Link to relevant villa and property listings when contextually natural.

### Content Notes

- Keep the comparison balanced. Do not position one island as universally better.
- Use Saint-Barthelemy in formal headings, with Saint-Barts as a parenthetical or secondary phrase where natural.
- Avoid unsupported claims about price, legal rules, or investment returns.
- Include a short disclaimer that buyers should seek legal and tax advice for transaction-specific questions.

## Production Checklist

### Before Writing

- Confirm whether the recommended URLs should be new pages or upgrades to existing WordPress pages/taxonomy archives.
- Confirm which Saint-Martin villa listings and property listings should be featured.
- Confirm whether Saint-Barthelemy and Anguilla have active listings, advisory coverage, or inquiry-only coverage.
- Gather any approved proof points beyond "Founded in 2019."

### On-Page Requirements

- Add exactly one H1 per page.
- Write unique title tags and meta descriptions.
- Use descriptive image alt text for meaningful images.
- Add FAQs that answer real pre-inquiry questions.
- Include clear inquiry CTAs above the fold and near the bottom of each page.
- Add contextual internal links between the three Month 1 content assets.
- Keep all body copy and metadata in English.

### Quality Guardrails

- Do not publish thin location or service pages with only listing excerpts and forms.
- Do not submit low-value or unfinished pages in the XML sitemap.
- Do not invent proof metrics, sales volume, review counts, or booking performance.
- Do not mix French body copy into English SEO pages.
- Do not let the blog become a broad travel guide that fails to connect back to rentals and property sales.

## Suggested Publishing Order

1. Publish or upgrade **Luxury Villa Rentals in Saint-Martin** first, because it supports the most immediate rental inquiry intent.
2. Publish **Luxury Real Estate in Saint-Martin, Saint-Barthelemy & Anguilla** second, because it clarifies the sales/property hub and supports buyer and seller leads.
3. Publish the comparison blog third, then use it to link readers into the two commercial pages.

## Success Measures

Track these once Search Console, analytics, and WordPress access are available:

- Pages indexed and submitted intentionally in the XML sitemap.
- Impressions and clicks for Saint-Martin villa rental queries.

- Organic entrances to the two commercial pages.
- Inquiry, contact, availability request, or booking actions from the new pages.
- Internal clicks from the blog article to the villa rental and real estate pages.
- Search snippets showing controlled title tags and meta descriptions.
